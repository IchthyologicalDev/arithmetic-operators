EXERCISE TEMPLATE

Description

Welcome to the Template Exercise! This exercise doesn't cover anything, since it's a template! You should use this as a basis for vanilla JS exercises. Read through the various files to get a sense for how everything works.

Instructions

  1. Open up index.js in your editor.
  2. Follow the instructions in the comment at the top of that file.
  3. Refresh this page after saving changes to see your progress.

Questions

My calculator is broken. When I try to do 5 + 5, it returns 55.

In my fishing guide service, I use a unique way of calculating tips. I round every guide's tips down to the nearest 10, and give the excess to the boat cleaner.

I took out a couple interest only loans to launch this guide service. I think that I'm being overcharged. Help me out! If I just pay the interest amount, what should my monthly payments be?

I can't wrap my head around tax brackets. What's my actual tax percentage?

I like helping out other small business owners. I gave someone $1000 to help launch their pizza company. We haven't worked out the interest rate yet, but they guaranteed that they will pay it back in full 1 year later. How much interest will this person me?

I hand out coupons for 50% off the next fishing trip to anyone who fails to catch a fish. How much should I charge someone who is using one of these coupons?

I have 1 bait bucket per client. I want an equal amount of bait in each bucket at the start of the day. How many pieces of bait will be left over after placing an equal amount in each bucket?

I get calls when the fishing heats up in certain areas. Can I make it to the new location within 30 minutes?

The pizza restaurant owner just informed me that they might need to wait longer before paying me back. How much will they owe me?
